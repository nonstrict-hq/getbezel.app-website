Frequently Asked Questions

How does the license work?

When you purchase Bezel, you can activate it on a single Mac. You keep the version you bought forever. You also receive one free year of updates and new features. Your purchased version of Bezel will never expire or deactivate.

Can I get an invoice for my purchase?

After succesful purchase, you get an email from Paddle with the invoice attached. If you need your business details and/or VAT number on the invoice, add VAT information in the second step during checkout.

Where can I find my license?

After a successful purchase, you get an email from us with your unique license key. Make sure to check your Spam folder.

If you can't find your license key please reach out to us at team@nonstrict.com.

How do I transfer my license to another Mac?

Deactivate the Mac currently using your license by going to Settings and selecting the License tab in the app. After deactivating you can use your license key on another Mac.

If you've lost access to the machine using your license, please contact us at team@nonstrict.com.

How do I purchase multiple licenses?

You can purchase multiple licenses at once in the checkout. There currently is no automatic discount in place when purchasing multiple licenses.

If you want to purchase 10 or more licenses at once please reach out to team@nonstrict.com so we can get you a discount.
