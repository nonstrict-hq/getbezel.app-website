Privacy Policy

This is the privacy policy of Nonstrict B.V. ("Nonstrict"). It was last changed on January 29, 2024.

Who is responsible for the processing of your personal data?

Nonstrict B.V. (Australiëring 33, Amersfoort, trade register number 89067657) is the controller of personal data within the meaning of the General Data Protection Regulation ("GDPR") for the processing activities listed below in this privacy statement.

What's Nonstrict's approach regarding privacy?

Nonstrict is very committed to respecting your privacy.

Nonstrict's business model is based on creating great services and products, not on widespread collection and marketing of user data. This means we'll only collect data we need to deliver our products and services to you, and to learn how to make it work better for you.

As such we aim to process as little personal data as possible, and to be as transparent as possible about the data that we do collect.

Our control only goes so far as the product and/or service we provide: please be aware that as a user, you are responsible for data you record or mirror.

How long does Nonstrict retain your personal data?

Personal data will solely be kept for as long as necessary. The retention period depends on the purposes for which Nonstrict has collected the personal data as well as any legal obligations concerning the storage of personal data.

What kind of data is stored? (Cookies)

Nonstrict uses local storage meganisms to store some information necessary to deliver the service and/or products requested by you.

The local storage meganisms (such as, but not limited to, cookies, browser local storage and user defaults) are small text files that are stored on your computer, smartphone or other device when you visit our website or use our apps. This locally stored data helps us to operate and analyze our service. We are the only party that make use of this data, therefore we also only use first party cookies (cookies placed by us).

These local storage meganisms do not allow us to access your computer, smartphone or other device, nor do they allow us to access any information other than that for which you have given us permission. We only make use of functional and analytical data with no or limited impact on your privacy. We do not make use of tracking-, marketing- or social media cookies. You can adjust your web browser or device settings to refuse use of certain storage meganism, such as cookies. As a result, it is possible that our products and/or services are no longer fully functional.

What are your rights?

The GDPR provides you the right of access, rectification and erasure of your personal data. In addition, you can ask us for restriction of processing, to transfer the personal data and you have the right to object to the processing. You can exercise these rights by contacting us via team@nonstrict.com. If you have a complaint, you can contact us via team@nonstrict.com. You also have the right to lodge a complaint with the Dutch supervisory authority, the Autoriteit Persoonsgegevens.

Does Nonstrict transfer personal data to third parties?

Nonstrict may use the services of third parties to process your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Statement. Such third parties will thereby act as a processor for Nonstrict and we ensure that they offer sufficient safeguards as regards security measures and accurate and lawful processing of personal data.

When you pay for our products or services, your payment will be effectuated by Paddle for purchases on our website or Apple for purchases made in the App Store. Paddle and Apple act as the Merchant of Record and will need to comply with regulatory obligations on their own. Therefore they have their own Terms and Privacy Policies.

In all other respects, Nonstrict only provides data to third parties subject to your prior consent or if we are obliged to do so under laws and regulations, or if we are required to do so as a result of legal proceedings and/or if we deem this necessary to protect our own interests or the interests of third parties.

Does Nonstrict transfer personal data outside of Europe?

Nonstrict aims to solely process your personal data within the EEA. Other personal, such as purchase data, will only be stored by Nonstrict or by third parties engaged by Nonstrict or transferred outside the European Union (and the EEA) if this is in accordance with the applicable legislation regarding the transfer of personal data to countries outside of the European Union.

What happens in the event of a transfer of the company?

In the future, one or more of Nonstrict's parts or assets may be transferred to a third party or Nonstrict may merge with a third party. In that case, your personal data can be transferred to this third party. You will be informed of such a transfer.

Can Nonstrict make changes to this privacy statement?

We may amend this privacy statement from time to time.

How can you contact us?

If you have any questions or comments regarding this privacy statement, please contact us at team@nonstrict.com.
